完型填空

发表于 2023-08-28 更新于 2023-09-22 分类于 考研

做题方法

  1. 精读全文首句，首句不设空，且跟主题紧密相关。
  2. 语法角度分析句子，确定空格成分。
  3. 文意角度分析句子，推测空格含义，上下文寻找一致信息（线索感），找到最佳答案（不是正确答案）。

语法

it is/was + … + that + … 强调句,可以直接把it is/was that 扛掉，去看剩余部分

单词/词组

by

  • A is brought by B (因果) B导致A
  • A is followed by B (时间先后) B随A而来
  • A is stimulated by B (因果) B导致A
  • A is characterized by B (无明显逻辑关系) A的特征是B

if

  • even if
    He has determined to prove his innocence,even if he has to go to the highest court in the land.
  • if only
    The pills might have helped him,if only he’d taken them regularly.
  • only if
    Only if a teacher has given permission is a student allowed to leave the room.
  • as if
    He behaved as if nothing had happened.

be

  • be advanced by 被推进、被发展（由某人或某事物推动）
  • be caught by 被抓住、被迷住（某人或某事物）
  • be bound by 受束缚、受约束（受到限制或约束）
  • be founded by 被创立、由…创立

as

  • view A as B 将A视为B
  • envision A as B 将A视为B
  • rate A as B 对A评价为B
  • define A as B 定义A为B
  • think A as B 将A思考为B
  • regard A as B 将A视作B
  • consider A as B 将A视为B
  • dismiss A as B 不予考虑A为B

    until

  1. 当主句谓语动词是延续动词时，通常用肯定形式。
  2. 若主句谓语动词是短暂动词，则要用否定形式。not..until..“直到…才”
  3. not..until…的四种不同句式：
    1. 正常结构
    2. until置于句首
    3. 装结构
    4. 强调结构

capability vs capacity

  • capability: the natural ability,skill,or power that makes a machine,person,or organization able to do something,especially something difficult 能力，才能
  • storage capability: 存储能力
  • capacity: the amount of space a container,room etc.has to hold things or people 能力，容量，产量，负载量，容纳力可以兼指无生命力事物的容积容量

    context contest contact contract contrast

  • contest: n. 背景，环境；上下文，语境
  • contact:
    • n. 联系，联络；接触，触摸；联络人，熟人，社会关系；会见，往来，接触；传染病接触者；隐形眼镜；接通电，触点，接头；（无线电）通信
    • v. 联系，联络；接触
    • adj. 供联络的；接触性的，通过接触而起作用的
  • contract:
    • n. 合同，契约
    • v.（使）收缩，缩小；签合同，订立契约；患上，感染；订立婚约（或盟约）；欠负（债）
  • contrast:
    • n. 差异，对比；对照物，明显不同的事物；（摄影或绘画中的）颜色反差，明暗对比；（电视屏幕的）图像明暗对比度，反差
    • v. 对比，对照；显出明显的差异，形成对比

Comparison

Comparisons were drawn between A and B 人们比较了A和B

give

  • give thought to sth 认真考虑某事
  • give sb an idea of sth 使某人明白了解想到
  • give an opinion on/about/of 对某事发表意见
  • give advice on/about 给出关于某事的建议

rarely & barely

  • rarely = not often,seldom,“不常，很少”是频率副词，只能修饰动词或者具有
    动词意味的形容词，不修饰-able后缀的表状态的形容词。
    I rarely see any high-school kids here.
    我很少看到高中生来这里。

  • barely = only just;almost not 仅仅，几乎不
    The music was barely audible.
    音乐声勉强能听见。
    There was a barely perceptible pause.
    出现了一个难以觉得的停顿。

of

  • fond of doing sth.喜爱做某事
  • fearful of doing sth.担心或害怕做某事
  • capable of doing sth.有能力做某事
  • thoughtless of doing sth.做某事考虑不周或轻率

keep

  • keep order 保持秩序
  • keep track of 保持知晓.的信息，与..保持联络
  • keep hold of 抓住；保持对..的控制
  • keep pace with 与….并驾齐驱，齐头并进

法律词汇

  • just 公正的，公平的=right + acceptable
  • justice 正义，公正
  • judge 法官，裁判员，判断
  • jury 陪审团
  • judiciary 司法机关，司法体系

考研中一些被动表达的固定翻译：

  • be exposed to面对，面临
  • be confronted with面对，面临
  • be forced to不得不
  • be tempted to禁不住，情不自禁地
  • be meant to打算，有意做，旨在
  • be entitled to有权做.有资格做.
  • be subjected to受到经历了.
  • be deprived of剥夺了.
  • be implicated in与.有牵连
  • be swamped by和.比起来，.显得相形见绌
  • be intertwined with = be closely connected with与.交织在一起，与.缠绕在一起

否定逻辑是考研完型的常考考点：

  • 2011:In fact,instead of straining muscles to build them as exercise does, laughter apparently accomplishes the opposite.
  • 2013:In theory,the success of an applicant should not depend on the few others chosen randomly for interview during the same day, but Dr Simonsohn suspected the truth was otherwise.

条件逻辑是考研完型的常考考点：

  • 2010:So long as something was changed,productivity rose.
  • 2012:The court cannot maintain its legitimacy as guardian of the rule of law when justices behave like politicians.
  • 2013:He theorized that a judge fearful of appearing too soft on crime might be more likely to send someone to prison if he had already sentenced 5 or 6 other defendants only to forced community service on that day.

归纳-演绎逻辑：

  1. 大宇宙归纳演绎（篇章展开的方式）：文章首段往往是对全文主题的概括性阐述。从第二段开始，每段讨论全文主题下的一个分支主题。首段有时会进行必要的铺垫，即引子，最后会在段未重申主题，或者会在第二段点明主题。
  2. 小宇宙归纳演绎（段落展开的方式）：段落的主题通常会在段落靠前的句子中提出，余下的句子则通过罗列具体论据的方式进行详细阐述偶尔会在段落末句对全段进行再一次总结
  3. 归纳-演绎逻辑：8 ways to present examples of a general thing
  • and other types of X
  • such X as
  • X such as
  • X including
  • X like
  • X:for example,
  • X that included
  • X(eg,…)

    让步逻辑：

  • 表让步转折关系的介词，副词：despite, in spite of, even , for all, notwithstanding
  • though, although, even though, even if, while, as
  • no matter+ what , who, when, where, which, how whatever, whoever, whenever, wherever, whichever, however

    主语从句（回顾2010完形讲解）

    eg: That the earth turns around the sun is known to all.
  • It is known to all that the earth turns around the sun.
  • It is necessary ( that…)
  • It is known to all ( that… )
  • It is common knowledge ( that…)
  • It occurred to me ( that… )
  • It turns out ( that… )
